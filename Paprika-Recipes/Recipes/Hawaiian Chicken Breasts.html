Hawaiian Chicken Breasts

Entree

Prep Time: Marinating time: 4 hours to overnight Servings: 2 Source: thehow.loseit.com

Ingredients

½ cup fresh pineapple chunks

⅓ cup lite coconut milk

1 1-inch piece fresh ginger, peeled and sliced 2 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves.

Directions

In a food processor or blender, combine pineapple, coconut, and ginger, and process until smooth. Reserve 1/4 cup of marinade.
Place chicken in a shallow dish and cover with remaining marinade, turning to coat.

Cover chicken and marinate in the refrigerator 4 hours to overnight.

Prepare grill. Remove chicken from marinade and grill, brushing with excess marinade, 5-6 minutes per side, or until chicken is cooked through.

Notes

Side suggestions: Coconut rice. Steamed snow peas.

Nutrition

Per serving:
185 calories
8 g carbohydrate
27 g protein
4.5 g fat (2.5 g sat)
0 g fiber
